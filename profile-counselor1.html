Dr. Tunde Adebayo

Trauma & Abuse Counselor | 15+ Years Experience

Biography

Dr. Tunde Adebayo is a dedicated Nigerian counselor specializing in trauma recovery and abuse-related therapy. With over 15 years of experience, he has helped countless individuals regain confidence and mental well-being. He holds a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from the University of Lagos and is a certified mental health advocate.
