About Us

Dedicated to providing support, guidance, and healing for individuals in need.

Our Mission

At Safe Haven Counseling, our mission is to empower individuals by offering professional guidance, emotional support, and practical solutions. We believe that no one should suffer alone, and we are here to help you rebuild your life with strength and confidence.

Meet Dr. Tunde Adebayo

Dr. Tunde Adebayo is a certified trauma and abuse counselor with over 15 years of experience in helping individuals recover from emotional and physical distress. With a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from the University of Lagos, Dr. Tunde is dedicated to providing evidence-based therapy tailored to each client’s unique needs.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We listen, we care, and we support without judgment.
  • Confidentiality: Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.
  • Empowerment: Helping individuals regain confidence and control over their lives.
  • Professionalism: A team of experts dedicated to high-quality counseling services.

What We Offer

Trauma Counseling

Helping survivors of abuse, violence, and trauma find emotional healing.

Relationship Therapy

Guidance for couples and families to build healthier relationships.

Legal & Emotional Support

Connecting clients with legal resources and emotional stability strategies.

Why Choose Us?

At Safe Haven Counseling, we go beyond just listening. We provide actionable solutions, professional care, and a compassionate space for you to heal. Whether you need trauma therapy, legal support, or emotional guidance, we are here for you.
